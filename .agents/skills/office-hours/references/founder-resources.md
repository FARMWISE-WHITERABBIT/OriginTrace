# Founder and Builder Resources

Use two or three resources in Phase 6 of the `office-hours` handoff. Match the
resource to the user's context and avoid repeats when prior profile data is
available.

## Garry Tan Videos

1. **My $200 million startup mistake: Peter Thiel asked and I said no** (5 min)
   The strongest "take the leap" story: Garry says no to a Peter Thiel check
   because a promotion feels safer. https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dtnG0ELjvcM

2. **Unconventional Advice for Founders** (48 min, Stanford)
   A broad pre-launch founder talk: psychology, good ideas that look bad, and
   growth from tiny starts. https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Y4yMc99fpfY

3. **The New Way To Build A Startup** (8 min)
   The 2026 AI startup playbook and the small-team "20x company" thesis.
   https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rWUWfj_PqmM

4. **How To Build The Future: Sam Altman** (30 min)
   Sam on going from idea to reality, finding your people, and conviction.
   https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xXCBz_8hM9w

5. **What Founders Can Do To Improve Their Design Game** (15 min)
   Taste and craft as founder advantages. https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ksGNfd-wQY4
